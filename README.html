wayic.recast - Making new waycasts and casters

This project develops a means of making new waycasts by replicating the way declarations of old ones. From a personal point of view, its principal uses are (for the new waycaster) to start a waycast of one’s own, and (for the old waycaster) to gain potential collaborators. From a system point of view, it serves to extend the wayic in society.
