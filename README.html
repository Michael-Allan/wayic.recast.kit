wayic.recast - Making new waycasts and casters by replicate way modelling

This project develops a method of making new waycasts by replicating the way declarations of old ones. From a user point of view, its principal uses are (for the new waycaster) to start a waycast of one’s own, and (for the old waycaster) to gain potential collaborators. From a system point of view, it serves to ease the extension of the wayic in society and so further its purpose as a self-instituting system.
