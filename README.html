Extending the wayic in society

Here is project wayic.recast.kit. This project implements a method of way formation based on a recast kit. Downloadable from an original waycast where it is installed, a recast kit enables anyone to make a new waycast that (by default) takes the form of the original. Recast kits are a basic component of the wayic architecture, where they function as social extenders of the wayic in service to its purpose as a self-instituting system of aimed way formation.
